HackOMania 2019

Gamifying The Future

HackOMania is an annual 24-hour hackathon organised by GeeksHacking Community that aims to bring together tech enthusiasts, students and working professionals from various backgrounds to tackle real-world issues using awesome tech solutions.

The theme of this year is Gamification! So assemble your squad1, throw on your wizarding hats, and let's get cranking!

If you are still unsure about participating, you can come down to the pre-event and find out more. You can register for pre-event here.

Follow our Facebook page for latest news!

1A team must have at most 5 members.

11 Golden Rules

  1. The hackathon is open to all.
  2. You may join as an individual or as a team of up to 5 members.
  3. At least 1 team member must be present at the event venue throughout the hackathon.
  4. Projects must seek to address at least 1 of the provided challenges.
  5. Each participant is responsible for the sourcing and usage of data and systems in their Hackathon projects.
  6. Each participant owns the intellectual property they generate during the Hackathon.
  7. All projects must be finalised during the Hackathon.
  8. Participants must stop work on their projects once time is up.
  9. All teams will be given 2 minutes to present during the semi-finals. 5 finalists will then be shortlisted and given 5 minutes to present in the finals.
  10. Participants are not allowed to bring pre-coded work.
  11. Participants may be disqualified from the competition at the organisers’ discretion (reasons include but are not limited to: breaking the rules, or other unsporting behavior).
