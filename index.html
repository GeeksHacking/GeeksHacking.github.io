Our Past HackOMania

"We are getting bigger!"

2018 - Creating Smart Homes

With the advent of the Internet of Things (IoT) and the ubiquitous mobile phone, the home surprisingly remains largely unchanged. We envision that through HackOMania 2018, we would be able to synergize the conveniences that tech has already given us with everyday use cases; creating solutions using machine learning and AI that would impact our everyday life at home

2017 - Social Networks

We are all social beings and with the advent of social networks, we are now more connected then ever before. Geeks came together on the 4th to 5th of March 2017 utilizing APIs from 4 major social networks to develop meaningful applications. The participants battled laptop crashes, a sleep deprived night and APIs going down to deliver applications that enhanced social interaction. Even if just in a small way, the teams sought to change the world with code. A total of $900 in cash was given away to winning teams. Here is what the winning team did during the hackathon.

2016 - Smart Nation

In line with the Smart Nation trust that Singapore is promoting, developers took part in HackOMania 2016, a 24hr hackathon from the 18-19 of March 2016 aimed at building meaningful projects focusing on the Smart Nation theme. Participants used public APIs via data.gov.sg to change the world with code. A total of $900 worth of prizes were given away.
